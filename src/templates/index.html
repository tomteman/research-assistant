Welcome to Research Assistant - your personalized Google Scholar.


This site allows you to keep track of your academic reading and research, and cooperate with colleagues.
  • Search for articles (just as you would search in Google Scholar)
  • Add labels and comments to articles - select a name for the label ( "TODO", "for Department Seminar", "Nice" etc...) and add comments to remember your thoughts.
  • Work with colleagues - working on a paper together? Share all articles belonging to a label in one click. As your colleagues add articles to the label - you will see them as well.
  • Stay updated via email - easily create an automated alert service ("Follow") for a specific author/article/journal/etc.

Get started by signing in using your Gmail account (don't have an account? create one here).
